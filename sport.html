Sports in Nelson Mandela Park

In 2014, Amsterdam Zuid Oost decided to pay tribute to Nelson Mandela by renaming the park originally built in 1969 after him. Extensive renovations were carried out to rejuvenate the park. Unveiling our comprehensive range of sport activities within this park, offering you the chance to explore its beauty.

We ensure that the recreational and athletic amenities within this lush green park are impeccably maintained, fully equipped, and easily accessible to all. Our commitment extends to offering top-notch sports programs and fostering a warm, inclusive social atmosphere for the athletes we serve.

We are Nelson Mandela Park! We love to play in Nature's Playground.
